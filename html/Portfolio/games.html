Kirby's Adventure remake

This was the first project for my Game Design class. I made it with a partner, and we had two weeks to re-make a classic game and to design a custom level. This is the result, it is a bit rough. I worked on the animations, and movement and transitions of the enemies and Kirby, and their attack powers. My partner worked on the physics of Kirby. We made this using the Unity game engine.
