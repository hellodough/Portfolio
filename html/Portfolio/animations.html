Turnip Head Animation

This is an animation I made in collaboration with many other artists on hitrecord.org. Based on a poem, I used artwork and music from other users and put together this animation in Adobe After Effects. My animation went on to be the base for another animation that was directed by Joseph Gordon-Levitt and narrated by Gary Oldman which then screened at Sundance 2012.

King Midas Tiny Animation

This is an animation I made in collaboration with many other artists on hitrecord.org. I animated a graphic from another user on the site and set it to music by another user using Adobe After Effects and Adobe Photoshop.

1000 true fans

This is a video I made for my Informatics class to explain Kevin Kelly's theory of 1000 true fans as well as Chris Anderson's Long Tail theory. I animated it in Adobe After Effects, made my own graphics using Adobe Photoshop and narrated it use Apple's Garageband.
