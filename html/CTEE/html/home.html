Volunteer

Need hours for NHS? Have some spare time? Want to look good for colleges? Or do you just like helping people? West Ottawa High School students have a unique opportunity to lend a hand within their own community, as well as helping those around the world.

Mission

It is our desire to help create a world in which each student is able to learn to the best of their abilities, and has the opportunity to meet whatever goals they may want to achieve.

Play a show

If you are a musician/performer/artist, you have the opportunity to perform on stage to hundreds of people at our annual music festival, WOstock. The money raised at this event goes towards shipping computers abroad to schools in developing countries so that everyone can have an equal opportunity for learning.
