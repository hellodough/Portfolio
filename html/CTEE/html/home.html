Mission

Technology is quickly advancing and without it, you fall behind. We want to help students in developing countries have access to computers so they too can learn. We also want to give high school students the opportunity to help out first-hand and to develop skills in planning events and technical computer skills.

Play a show

If you are a musician/performer/artist, you have the opportunity to perform on stage to hundreds of people at our annual music festival, WOstock. The money raised at this event goes towards shipping computers abroad to schools in developing countries so that everyone can have an equal opportunity for learning.
