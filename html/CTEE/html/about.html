About

Which is shorthand for Cultural Technological Environmental Exchange. We are a non-profit organization at West Ottawa High School whose goal is to help others. We do this by shipping thousands of computer stations to schools in Cameroon so that they can learn without technological limitations. Every year we put on a two-day music festival with over 60 local bands, as well as running an annual canned-food drive around the holidays. We are always giving.
